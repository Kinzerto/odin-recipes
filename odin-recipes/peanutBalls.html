Easy Peanut Butter Energy Balls

Home
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup crushed walnuts
  • ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • ¼ cup honey, or more as needed
  1. Beat oats, peanut butter, walnuts, chocolate chips, and honey in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment until well combined, adding more honey if mixture is not holding together.
  2. Scoop mixture with a cookie scoop and form into 24 balls; place on a cookie sheet. Refrigerate until firm, 30 to 60 minutes.
